The Road Trip

1 Becher Gare, 6230 Bech, Luxembourg

Enjoy a magical place, for you who love good food, and countryside.

In an enchanting setting, the restaurant (former Buffet from Bech station) located on top of the hill, with a view on the valley, with the lovely village of Bech below.

Open menu

10 minutes from Junglinster, 6 minutes from Echternach, the restaurant Becher Gare invites you for a journey that combines culinary experience, breath-taking views and a warm welcome.

A large car park, 3 terraces, the valley on one side, the forest on the other, it is a must to stop whether you are on a road trip, coming as a neighbour or simply for the pleasure of the ride.

The Chef and his second in command

Welcome to La Becher Gare! I am Frederic, the Chef and I am passionate about the authenticity and quality of products. I'm proud to work here with Tom, my second in command in the kitchen, a native of the region.

For us, authentic cuisine is much more than just a job. It's a real passion, a way of conveying emotions and telling stories through every dish we prepare. I attach great importance to the selection of the best ingredients, favouring local and seasonal products. This approach guarantees the freshness and the tastiness of our dishes, while supporting local producers.

Tom, being Luxembourgish native, has a deep knowledge of the products and culinary traditions of his land. His expertise perfectly complements my creativity and together we are constantly challenging ourselves.

Being inspired by traditional recipes, we like adding a personal and contemporary touch.

We look forward to welcoming you and sharing our passion.

Our Menu

Our menu changes monthly with the seasons. It is available from Thursday to Saturday, and Sunday evening.

On Sunday lunchtime, a single menu-carte is offered including Amuse-Bouche; 4 starters to choose from; Sorbet; Fish and meat of your choice (4 proposals) and 4 choices of desserts for a price of 63 euros.

A Vegetarian and Vegan choice is always available, do not hesitate to call us to create your group menus, or others events.
