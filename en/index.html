Meet Frédéric

Frédéric, the owner and head chef of Becher Gare, has a passion for both cooking and classic cars.

Born and raised in France, Frédéric developed an early appreciation for the beauty and simplicity of French cuisine.

After years of training and experience in some of the best kitchens in Europe, Frédéric migrated to Luxembourg to take over Becher Gare, a restaurant that showcases the best of French and Luxembourgish cuisine in the heart of Europe.

Frédéric's culinary philosophy is simple: use the highest quality ingredients to create dishes that are both delicious and authentic. When he's not in the kitchen, Frédéric can often be found tinkering with his classic cars or exploring the Luxembourgian countryside on a road trip.

Join Frédéric at Becher Gare for a unique dining experience that combines his love of food and classic cars in a beautiful rural setting.

Our Menu

At Becher Gare, our menu showcases the best of French and Luxembourgian cuisine, with a focus on fresh, seasonal ingredients.

From traditional favorites like escargots and coq au vin, foie gras to vegan options, our menu has something for everyone.

Don't forget to ask our knowledgeable staff for wine pairing suggestions to enhance your dining experience.
